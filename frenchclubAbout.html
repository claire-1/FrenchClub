We are the Berkeley High School French Club. We meet once a week to participate in French oriented activites and fundraise for Medecins San Frontier. We believe it is important to expose people to French culture. We will be doing this by speaking French, watching French oriented movies, eating French food and supporting French speaking countries! Anyone can join the club. We meet every Tuesday at lunch in room C102. See our calendar for upcoming events.
